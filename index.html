My journey begins at the intersection of technology and humanity. As the digital divide expands, native language barriers and isolated rural settings prevent millions of talented young minds from seizing the potential of artificial general intelligence (AGI).

As a designer, LLM curator, and builder, I construct practical tools that translate complex algorithmic processes into localized dialects. Backed by community action, we establish solar-powered mesh platforms and learning labs straight in the hearts of remote Pakistani regions.

My work centers on the synthesis of Urdu NLP toolkits, curriculum creation, and founder-level partnerships that convert technical education into direct, real-world micro-economies.
